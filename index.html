Pharmacy Student Organization Tools

Outlook Calendar

Outlook calendar is a great tool for students. It has the option to add multiple calendar categories. This allows students to easily organize tasks, class time and meetings. With Outlook, you have the ability to put in Zoom links for virtual courses and other appointments into your calendar.

Other Features
  • Check the availability of participants prior to scheduling an appointment
  • Create appointments from an email
  • Easily schedule teams meetings for appointments in app

    • The COP provides access to Outlook with your pharmacy email. This product can be accessed from its website or its app on PC or Mac.

    OneNote

    OneNote is another tool provided by UA COP. This application allows students to easily store and notate on lecture slides provided by instructors.

    Other Features
  • Separtate files by class and exam with notebooks, sections and pages
  • Store lectures in the cloud and avoid taking up space on your device
  • Add text or markings to lecture slides
  • Access lectures from any device

    • This resource can be accessed when downloading office for student use from the UA. OneNote also has device specific apps for PC or Mac.

    Trello

    Trello is an excellent task managment program that can help ensure that you are on top of your homework, projects and other tasks. It is a visually pleasing application that allows users to create, prioritize and schedule specific tasks.

    Other Features
  • Set due dates for specific tasks
  • Create a checklist for larger tasks that required multiple steps
  • Share Trello boards with other people to complete tasks as a group
  • Create a Trello "card" directly from Outlook

    • Trello is a free resource. If you need help creating a board, I have attached a template here. Trello also has device specific apps for PC or Mac.

    Office 365

    Office 365 is a hub of resources provided to COP students. It contains all of the office resources in a place that can be easily shared and stored in the cloud.

    Other Features
  • Create powerpoints that you can easily access on any device
  • Share your CV with your mentor for realtime updates
  • Store documents in the cloud
  • Create group folders for important projects to allow for simultaneous editing

    • This resource can be accessed here using your pharmacy username and password.

    Self Control

    Self Control is an application that restricts specific programs and websites for a specified time. This can be helpful for students that have a hard time maintaining focus during their study time.

    Other Features
  • Block your own access to specific distracing websites
  • Prevent emails notification
  • Self select time durations
  • Implement the Pomodoro Technique into your study routine

    • Unfortunatley this application is only available on Mac, but there should be similar applications available for pc.
