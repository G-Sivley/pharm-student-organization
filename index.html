Student Organization Resources

Outlook Calendar

Outlook calendar is a great tool for students. It has the option to add multiple calendar categories. This allows students to easily organize tasks, class time and meetings. With Outlook, you have the ability to put in Zoom links for virtual courses and other appointments.

Other Features
  • Create appointments while checking for availability of other participants
  • Associate emails with an appointments
  • Easily schedule teams meetings for appointments in app

    • The COP provides access to Outlook with your pharmacy email. This product can be accessed from its website or its app on PC or Mac.

    OneNote

    OneNote is another tool provided by UA COP. This application allows students to easily store and notate on lecture slides provided by instructors.

    Other Features
  • Separtate files by class and exam with notebooks, sections and pages
  • Store lectures in the cloud and avoid taking up space on your device
  • Add text or markings to lecture slides
  • Access lectures from any device

    • This resource can be accessed when downloading office for student use from the UA. OneNote also has device specific apps for PC or Mac.
